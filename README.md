## What is Cymmetri?

Cymmetri is a **Converged Identity & Access Management Platform** and a well-trusted platform acting as an advisor and an end-to-end partner for Security-aware teams looking to deploy Identity and Access Management Solutions across their organization.&#x20;

We offer an industry-standard product backed by a strong team that always aims to innovate our solutions to cater to a wide variety of enterprise needs.
