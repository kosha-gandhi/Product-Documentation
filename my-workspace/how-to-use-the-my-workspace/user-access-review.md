# User Access Review

The Access Review provides users with a comprehensive suite of tools to manage and monitor campaign reviews. This module is designed to facilitate the assessment of current campaigns, provide visibility into completed reviews, and allow Campaign Managers to specifically track campaigns under their direct purview.

* Active Access Review&#x20;

This section allows the approving authority to select and certify any active campaign. This feature streamlines the certification process by enabling focused attention on campaigns currently in progress.&#x20;

* Completed Access Review&#x20;

Users can access a consolidated view of all completed reviews in this section. It offers a comprehensive overview of finalized assessments, enabling efficient tracking and easy reference.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (65).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Managed Access Review&#x20;

1. Specifically designed for Campaign Managers, this section displays all reviews where the user is designated as the campaign manager. This targeted view facilitates quick identification and management of campaigns under the user's responsibility, enhancing overall accessibility and control.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (66).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. Campaign Managers can also reassign a review task to another user, such as a manager or supervisor. This functionality is crucial in situations where the originally assigned reviewer is unavailable due to unforeseen circumstances, ensuring continuity and flexibility in the review process.
